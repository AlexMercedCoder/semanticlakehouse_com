---
title: "Data Lakehouse"
description: "An open data architecture that combines the flexibility of a data lake with the management features of a data warehouse."
related: ["apache-iceberg", "semantic-layer"]
---

A **Data Lakehouse** is a modern, open data architecture that enables you to store all your data in a cost-effective data lake (like Amazon S3 or ADLS) while providing the transactional guarantees and performance typically associated with a data warehouse.

This architecture eliminates the need for complex, costly ETL pipelines that move data from lakes to warehouses. Dremio provides a robust engine and catalog designed specifically to realize the full potential of the Data Lakehouse architecture.
