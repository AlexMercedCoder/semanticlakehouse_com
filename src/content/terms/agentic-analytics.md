---
title: "Agentic Analytics"
description: "The application of autonomous AI agents to perform complex data analysis and workflow execution."
related: ["semantic-layer"]
---

**Agentic Analytics** represents the evolution from basic conversational BI to autonomous action. Instead of just answering questions, AI agents can formulate queries, interpret results, and orchestrate follow-up actions.

To function effectively and avoid hallucinations, these agents require a robust **Semantic Layer** that provides well-defined, consistent metrics and dimensions. Dremio's unified platform is uniquely positioned to empower these AI-driven workflows by providing instant, governed access to lakehouse data.
